How It Works

One loop, three outcomes

Contribution becomes currency, currency becomes essentials, and the record of it all becomes a way into work.

Earn

Do good, get paid

Street cleanups, helping a neighbour, finishing a nano-learning course — verified through peer review and converted into Zlto tokens.

Spend

Real essentials

Groceries, airtime, data, electricity — redeemable at over 3,000 partner stores across the country.

Build

A verified record

Every task logs to a digital CV — proof of consistency and skill that helps young people bridge into formal employment.
